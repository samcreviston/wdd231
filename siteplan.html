Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to store and present adventure content for Dungeon Masters (DMs) guiding players in Dungeons and Dragons (D&D). This embraces not only the playing of a fun social game, but simplifies the effort of creating content, making more time for people to come together and have fun.

Audience

The audience of this website are DMs, aspiring, beginning, and experienced alike. These DMs are likely busy in their life and don't have all the time in the world to create a whole fantasy world for their groups of 2+ (2-5 on average) players. These are commonly young and middle aged men.
